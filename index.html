The Challenge

Why Clean Cooking Matters

Across 30 African nations, nearly 79% of households depend on biomass fuels for cooking — causing devastating health, environmental, and economic impacts. Mission 300 aims to transform this through coordinated investment, policy reform, and technology deployment.
